Hi, I'm Sam!

I'm a junior at the University of Michigan majoring in Information Science (User Experience Design) and minoring in Creative Writing.

As a writer, I love stories that have twists to them. My biggest inspirations are Jhumpa Lahiri and Stephen King. I love the open-endedness of prose writing! The ambiguity of starting with a blank page is very much one of the best things about writing.

I'm passionate about all things interdisciplinary! I'd love to combine my background in writing and communication with my experience with coding, UX Research, and designing with accessibility in mind.
